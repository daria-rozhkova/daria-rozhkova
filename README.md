## Explored & retired
 
### F1 Race Decision Tree *(2024)*
An interactive app for F1 fans to explore race decisions —
click through alternate outcomes and see what could have happened
if a team had pitted earlier, used a different strategy, and so on.
 
**What I did:**
- Mapped the existing market (apps, sites, social, paid & free)
- Conducted user interviews with F1 fans across forums and chats —
  both newcomers and experienced followers
- Identified the core pain point: quality analysis exists,
  but almost everything is behind a paywall
**Why I stopped:**
F1 content is heavily licensed — building a free product on top of it
isn't viable. The market gap I saw was real, but the constraint was legal,
not technical.
 
**What I learned:**
How to run a lightweight discovery process before writing a single line of code.
